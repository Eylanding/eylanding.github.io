Synopsis

This stage of the game is also relatively idle, especially once autosupremacy is unlocked. The interesting part here is graduation routing and student distributions, which are already optimised to oblivion.

This part also has the infamous timewall, the 5k push at the end. Be prepared for a long push.

Other than that, not much to do here, but it's only gonna get more complicated form here on out.

Rundown

Estimated time:

A few days to a week or 2

What to do if you're stuck:

If you are stuck for more then a few days you are probrably going something wrong. Are you:

  • Using the correct autoprestige and autosupremacy formula? Using the incorrect autoprestige / supremacy formula can impede progress, so make sure to check. The formulae are here
  • Autosupremacy needs you to do a manual supremacy to get it working. You must re do this manual supremacy every time you open the equation menu. If you could afford some upgrades if you supremacied, you probably need to do a manual supremacy.
  • Supermacying in the right places? The general rule of thumb is to supremacy whenever you can afford an upgrade.
  • Buying / autobuying all variables/upgrades? With an autobuyer, or the buy all button, you can disable buying certain variables/upgrades, which would definitely impede progress.
  • Using the right student distribution? Use !sigma in the discord server or use the student optimiser. Read intro to grad for more info
  • Graduating in the right places? That grad route is 2k -> 4k -> 4.8k -> 5k
  • Underestimating the time taken? The 5k push can take upwards of a few days, and is a big timewall.
  • Asking for help in the discord server? Anything not mentioned here can almost always be answered by the amazing people on the discord server

Graduation route

2k -> 4k -> 4.8k -> 5k

Alternate: If you accidentally graduate at 4.6k, its faster to push to 5k than to graduate at 4.8k and than recover. This will make the 5k push harder though. If you accidentally graduate at 4.4k, graduate at 4.6k before going to 5k. If you accidentally graduate at 4.2k or below, graduate at 4.8k before 5k

Autosupremacy

This costs an enormous amount of stars. It is possible you only unlock it at the start of the next section. If so, please refer back here anyway

(d(ln(db / b + 1) / pt) < 0)> 3 * tr && db > b&& dpsi + psi > min(min(costUpS(1),costUpS(2)), costUpS(3))&& ln(1 + max(1, log10(sf)) /smooth(max(1, log10(gf)),(st > tr) * ee99)) / max(1, st) <smooth(ln(1 + max(1, log10(sf)) /smooth(max(1, log10(gf)), (st > tr)* ee99)) / max(1, st), (pt > tr) * ee99)

Remember to set your mode to equation

IMPORTANT! After you input this, do a manual supremacy. Anytime you enter the edit expression field (the place where you put this in) you have to do another manual supremacy or this will break Also, you have to have you autobuyers set to x1 or xMax

The push to 5k

When you're doing the push to 5k make sure to do the following:

  1. You must have 18 or 19 students for the push to 5k. If you have 17, than you should grad at 4.8k for 19 students. If you have 16, graduate at 4.6k for 18 students. If you have 15 or less, why are you thinking about doing the 5k push, do get more students and come back
  2. Get the correct autoprestige and autosupremcy formulae in and working. If you already have the correct autosupremacy formula do NOT open the equation window, just leave it as is.

    3. The final steps are the most Important

  3. Update your student distribution as you go, and not just at the beginning. If you prefer, ready made student distributions for the average run are below, though, your case may differ
  4. Update your permanent level star distribution at least once after passing 4.6k
  5. Mentally prepare for the long push (it may take a while)

Notes:

  • When you get to 4995 f(t) turn of auto-prestige, it's faster to just cruise to 5k than to prestige and recover
  • When you get past 4.7k, with the required amount of students, sell auto-supremacy. You have maxed out your supremacy upgrades, and upgrade 3 requires upwards of 9.2k ft to reach, so it serves no use now, and the extra 100k stars can be used for permanent levels. You will re-buy it after the push.
  • Never turn of your autobuy. This is because graduations are based off cumulative ft, so buying variables and upgrades can only make you faster.

Other miscellaneous info
